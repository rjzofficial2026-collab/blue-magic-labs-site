Capabilities & Sourcing

What we are building, and who we are looking for.

We are actively evaluating manufacturing and packaging partners across the fine fragrance supply chain. This page outlines our product scope and sourcing requirements at a level appropriate for initial supplier evaluation.

Fragrance Development

For fragrance houses, oil suppliers and independent perfumers.

Formulation partners

Fragrance houses and independent perfumers capable of fine fragrance concentrate development and small-batch production.

Formats

  • Eau de Parfum, 18–22% concentration
  • 10ml pocket format
  • 50ml and 100ml standard formats
  • Refillable bottle systems

Bottles & Components

For bottle manufacturers and component suppliers.

Glass & components

Bottle manufacturers and component suppliers (caps, pumps, sprayers) able to support both standard and semi-custom tooling.

Primary packaging

  • Primary glass packaging (standard and custom tooling)
  • Closures, pumps, and dosing mechanisms

Packaging & Print

For carton, print and finishing partners.

Packaging & print

Carton, print, and finishing partners able to meet premium retail packaging standards.

Secondary packaging

  • Secondary packaging (cartons, print, foiling)
  • Discovery / sampling set formats

Manufacturing / Filling

For manufacturing and filling partners across the production chain.

Manufacturing partners

Building long-term relationships with manufacturing and packaging partners capable of meeting our quality, compliance, and production standards.

Minimum order quantities

We plan initial production runs in line with standard industry MOQs for a new fine fragrance brand, and are able to discuss specific volume commitments directly with shortlisted partners.

Regulatory & Documentation

For regulatory specialists and compliance partners.

Compliance

Products are developed with IFRA fragrance standards, EU/UK cosmetic product regulation (including CPNP/OPSS notification and Responsible Person requirements), and REACH in mind from the formulation stage.

Development Readiness

Detailed specifications, target volumes, and timelines are shared directly with partners following an initial qualification conversation.
