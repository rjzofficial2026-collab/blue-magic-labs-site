What we do

We design and bring to market fine fragrance and personal care products, working across the full development chain from concept to finished product.

01

Fragrance Development

Concept-to-formula development in partnership with fragrance houses and independent perfumers, targeting fine fragrance (EDP) concentration and modern olfactive positioning.

02

Product & Packaging

Format and packaging development across pocket, standard, and refillable presentations, built for both retail shelf and direct-to-consumer channels.

03

Sourcing & Partnerships

Building long-term relationships with manufacturing and packaging partners capable of meeting our quality, compliance, and production standards.
