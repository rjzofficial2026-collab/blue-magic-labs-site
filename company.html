Company

A privately held fragrance development company built for prototype development and supplier partnerships.

Blue Magic Labs Ltd was established in the United Kingdom in 2026 to develop a new category of emotion-led fine fragrance and personal care products, built on modern fragrance chemistry and disciplined brand development.

Commercial foundation for product development

BLUE MAGIC LABS LTD is founder-led by Raoul Zenon, a senior commercial and operational strategist with experience across beauty, luxury, consumer health, e-commerce and regional commerce environments spanning Europe and the GCC.

That background shapes how the company works with suppliers and partners: structured briefs, realistic formulation and packaging requirements, compliance readiness considered from the earliest conversation, and commercial execution grounded in category experience rather than guesswork.

How we operate with partners

The operating model behind product development and partner engagement.

Founder-led decisions

Product direction, supplier selection and commercial priorities are led directly by the founder to keep development focused and accountable.

Partner-enabled development

BLUE MAGIC LABS works with selected external specialists, fragrance houses, packaging partners, manufacturers and regulatory advisors as the development pipeline progresses.

NDA-based disclosure

Full concept details, fragrance direction and manufacturing requirements are shared with shortlisted partners under NDA following an initial qualification conversation.

Development principles

Principles that guide how we build products and select partners.

Craft-led development

Every product is developed in collaboration with experienced perfumers and fragrance houses, not templated or outsourced blind.

Regulatory rigor

Products are developed with IFRA standards and applicable UK/EU cosmetic regulation in mind from the earliest formulation stage.
