Company

A privately held fragrance development company.

BLUE MAGIC LABS develops proprietary fine-fragrance concepts from product architecture and prototype development to market readiness, working with selected formulation, packaging, manufacturing and regulatory partners.

Founded and led by Raoul Zenon, Founder & Managing Director, with a commercial strategy shaped by experience across European and GCC markets.

Precision at speed

BLUE MAGIC LABS is built on precision, speed and disciplined execution.

Development principles
